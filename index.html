Quoridor Mini

Player 1's turn

Be the first player to reach the other side of the board. On your turn, you can do one of 3 moves:

  1. Move player 1 space orthogonally
  2. Place a wall anywhere on the map as long as it doesnt completely block a path
  3. Charge up your barge attack which skips your turn. On your next turn you can break one wall permanently
